Sweden Through Time
Sweden needed to stand out on the World stage.
CLIENTSvenska institutet

The Swedish Institute’s main task is to promote interest and trust in Sweden around the world. Our task was to give their ambassadors new and innovative ways to present the story of their countries past and present. Current Powerpoint presentations needed sprucing up. Sweden needed to stand out on the World stage.

Our delivery was a WebGL application, custom built from the ground up. The app enables Swedish Ambassadors around the world to present in an eye catching and completely fresh, new way.

The user can click, drag and interact with the low-poly style Islands, each representing a decade in Sweden’s history.
