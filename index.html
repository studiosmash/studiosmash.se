We are Studio Smash

Smash is a new kind of design studio that creates ethereal brand experiences. Combining 3D, with innovative technology and visual craftsmanship, we kindle emotions, we forge memories, we evoke wonder.

We have a new way of thinking about collaboration.

Smash is a collaborative network of experience designers coalescing around Stockholm, Sweden. We embrace people and perspectives, individuals and ideas and stand by the fact that a collective mind is greater than one. Forming organically around projects, we draw on our in-depth knowledge of multiple fields – from 3D websites to AR applications to projection mapping shows – creating bespoke and unique experiences that people can enjoy.

To create these moments, we have an obsessive attention to detail, use state-of-the-art technology and work closely with bold brands to turn unique ideas into profound realities.

Together, let’s Smash it.
